Our Courses

Door Supervisor

A door supervisor is a person who works in a premise and is authorized by a local governing body. The role of the door supervisor is to take responsibility for the security and the safety of the visitors and customers within the premises.

Enroll now!

Security Guards

We offer pre-employment skills training. The training sessions are primarily structured for those who wish to work as Security Guards in the security industry.It also extends to those who wish to be security guards and personnel that maintain order and ensure security in offices, homes and other institutions.

Enroll now!

Physical Skilling

Physical skills required for the door supervision training are covered in our training. Asides physicals, fitness examinations and body exercises, our training includes communication skills, etiquettes and manners and other moral requirements for the public or private security operative
