About Us

Welcome to Security Trainings
We Train You For Succcess

We are a training entity accredited by NOCN and NCFE which is an accredited organization known for its awards, professionalism and quality qualifications.

Integral to our operation is the organization of high standard certified training sessions with consent of reputable institutions for SMEs (small and medium-sized enterprises) alike, towards attaining an edge in competitive markets

We record a high success rate for all our students due to our strategic approaches to training them. At Education House Leeds, training sessions are structured to meet the individual needs of each student.

We organize a lot of training courses for our students. Some of them are:

  • Door security training
  • Functional skills (English language)
  • Mathematics
  • ICT

We organize trainings for those who wish to join security industry as a security guard or as a door supervisor. Via these trainings, we empower students with the necessary skillset required for maximum performance as security guards and door supervisors. Physical skilling, CCTV monitoring, Close up security and first aid are the major sections of sections training.

We also offer GCSE and A Level tuition alongside Functional skills in Math, English and ICT to train people trying to secure an admission into a University, those searching for jobs and apprenticeships, or students about to take a literacy test.
